SHIVA DISTILLERIES PRIVATE LIMITED, established in the year 1983 at Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, India, is engaged in the production of wide range of Indian Made Foreign Liquor, with an annual production capacity of 11.10 million cases.Some of the well known and highly preferred brands of connoisseurs are Cardinal No.1 VSOP Brandy, Classic Grandee Premium rum in the premium segment Cardinal Grape Brandy, Gold Maker White Rum, Oak Vat matured Rum, Arizona Premium Apple vodka,Cardinal No.1 Whisky and Cosmopolitan Whisky in the medium segment and Monitor in the regular segment.Ultra modern machinery coupled with efficient man power makes Shiva Distilleries Private Limited a force to reckon within the liquor market
